## What is Fivefive?

_Multiplayer Sequence — fast, smart, social. Play with friends or against bots, anywhere a browser opens._

The game is **Sequence** (trademark of Jax Ltd.); **Fivefive** is the platform.
Five in a row is the win condition, and five-five is the rhythm — two halves of a game, two players slapping the same chip-line, the high-five after a sequence locks.

## Naming notes

- **Trademark line**: "Sequence™ is a registered trademark of Jax Ltd. Fivefive is an unofficial platform for playing the game online."
- **Casing**: `fivefive` (one word, lowercase) in body copy. `Fivefive` (capitalized) only at the start of sentences. Never `FiveFive` or `Five Five`.
- **Domain**: play.fivefive.app
- **Email sender**: hello@fivefive.app (not `noreply@`)
